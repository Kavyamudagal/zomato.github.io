About Us

Welcome to Zomato, your go-to source for all things food-related. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of dining experiences, with a focus on user-friendliness, reliability, and deliciousness.

Founded in 2008 by Deepinder Goyal and Pankaj Chaddah, Zomato has come a long way from its beginnings in India. When Zomato first started out, the passion for connecting people with great food experiences drove us to develop a platform that serves millions of users worldwide.
